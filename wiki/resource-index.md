## Safety Checklist Before Using Any External Resource

- Confirm the link points to official GitHub pages, known maintainers, or trusted app stores.
- Confirm compatibility with CMF Phone 2 Pro and your current Nothing OS build.
- Keep a local backup before trying advanced tools.
- Avoid random mirror links for firmware or partition images.
